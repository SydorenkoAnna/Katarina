Island Hotel Katarina Rooms & Suites

Enjoy the beautiful view of the sea or park from one of the 62 rustically furnished rooms or 58 spacious suites. Ground level rooms lead straight to the park or to the beach, drawing you into the Mediterranean lifestyle.

All rooms and suites are equipped with:
  • SAT TV
  • Telephone
  • Wooden parquet flooring
  • Wi-Fi included in the price
  • Pets – not allowed
  • Mini bar – at request
  • Toilet/bathroom (shower/bathtub, hair dryer)
  • Air conditioning
  • Smoke alarm
  • Room / suite for disabled – not available
  • Safe
  • Balcony or terrace
  • Non smoking rooms
  • Baby cot on request (included in the price)
