Island Hotel Katarina Beaches & Pools

Surrounded by dozens of secluded beach spots, some rocky, others pebble Hotel Katarina makes for a relaxing time at the beach. Enjoy sunbathing next to the pool, with refreshments at hand.

Beaches

The eastern side of the island consists of cradled rocky coves that are frequented by guests. Zlatna (Golden), Srebrna (Silver) and Brončana (Bronze) cliffs on the western side of the island are better suited for lovers of jump diving.

  • 50 m distance from the hotel
  • Rocky, pebbles, children's beach
  • Sun-loungers and parasols included in the price

Outdoor pools

A cluster of pools, right in front of the hotel restaurant invite you for a lazy afternoon soaking and sunbathing.

  • Pool size:
    • large pool: 295,75 m2,
    • middle pool 178,15 m2,
    • children's pool 25 m2
  • Pool depth:
    • large and middle 1.60 m,
    • children's pool 0,2 m
  • Seawater
  • Whirlpool
  • Sun-loungers and parasols included in the price
