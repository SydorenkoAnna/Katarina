Island Hotel Katarina

Island Hotel Katarina

Katarina is the island closest to Rovinj where you can enjoy an exclusive stay in a retro-style historical hotel, swimming in a pool or hidden-away island coves, a lunch by the beach, sports activities, or walk through the Mediterranean gardens. After a 5-minute romantic boat trip, continue your stroll along the narrow streets of Rovinj’s old town centre.

Rooms & Suites

Enjoy the beautiful view of the sea or park from one of the 110 rustically furnished rooms or ten spacious suites.

Island Hotel Katarina Beaches & Pools

Surrounded by dozens of secluded beach spots, some rocky, others pebble Hotel Katarina makes for a relaxing time at the beach. Enjoy sunbathing next to the pool, with refreshments at hand.

The eastern side of the island consists of cradled rocky coves that are frequented by guests. Zlatna (Golden), Srebrna (Silver) and Brončana (Bronze) cliffs on the western side of the island are better suited for lovers of jump diving.
