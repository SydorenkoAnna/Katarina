Island Hotel Katarina Restaurants & Bars

Hotel restaurants have kept their Austro-Hungarian charm with wooden beam ceilings and a terrace leading to the pool.

National Restaurant

The a la carte restaurant at Hotel Katarina serves a buffet breakfast and dinner, as well as a la carte lunch and dinner. By all means, don't miss our themed dinners. On Mondays, we hold Barbecue Nights. Choose your own piece of meat, and our cooks will prepare it in front of you, exactly to your liking. For Wednesdays, we recommend the Count Milewski Evening. We go back in time to conjure up the specialties of his era. At our Fishermen’s Dinners on Fridays, guests can choose from a fresh catch of fish and enjoy a live klapa performance.

Bistro Plaža

Should you desire a relaxed break in casual clothes, come find us on the main beach in front of the hotel Katarina. The a la carte restaurant offers pizza, Mediterranean dishes and ice cream. You can also have a coffee or beer, wine or your favorite liquor to wind down on your holiday.

Lobby Bar Katarina

Find us at the hotel Katarina and relax at the snack bar over coffee, your favorite drink, sandwich or some other light snack. Guests can choose from the varied offer of refreshing and alcoholic beverages and their favorite cocktails. Make sure to drop by for dessert as well.

Photo gallery

Pool Bar Katarina

Relax at the hotel’s poolside bar. We offer a wide selection of coffees, freshly squeezed juices and ice cream. If you prefer a cold poolside beer, we got you covered.

Photo gallery

Fermata Caffè

Guests waiting for their boat at the Delfin Quay or taking a stroll down Rovinj's new promenade by the Grand Park Hotel Rovinj can seek refreshments in the wide selection of gins and spritzes, other alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages, and hot drinks. We also offer light bites and desserts.
